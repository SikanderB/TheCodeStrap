Frequently Asked Questions

We’ve got answers to some of your most common questions.

What are your operating hours? ▼

We are open Monday to Sunday from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Do I need to book in advance? ▼

Bookings are not required, but recommended to avoid waiting — especially on weekends.

Do you accept card payments? ▼

Yes, we accept Visa, Mastercard, and most major debit and credit cards.

Are your products or services suitable for children? ▼

Yes, we offer child-friendly services. Feel free to reach out if you have special requirements.

How can I contact customer support? ▼

You can reach us via WhatsApp, phone, or email — details are on our Contact page.
